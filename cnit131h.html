Sharing my travel experience

Japan

Kansai is the second-largest city in Japan. People who are living there are very friendly and polite. There are many famous sightseeing spots, such as USJ Universal Studios, the world's largest aquarium, and Osaka Kaiyukan.
The most popular tourist destination is Dotobori. a lot of neon lights are hanging on outside the building. it makes the city so bright and so pretty, especially at night time.

Beijing

Beijing is the capital of China. It is also one of the most populous and fastest-growing cities in China. When you arrive in Beijing, you can see how China has developed.
There are so many museums in Beijing, even spending 5 days there are still not enough. each tourist is so big and a lot of things check out.
